Lulu was born on October 6, 2013. She is a short-haired, red-headed, chihuahua; and yes, the Taco Bell chihuahua is her soulmate. Named after a popular League of Legends champion, she embodies the character's spunky attitude and adorable stature. Her favorite food is chicken, but she is not very picky! Her biggest phobia is abnormally large cats. Lulu enjoys smelling a variety of different scents. Some of those include: poop, rocks, and of course, chicken.
